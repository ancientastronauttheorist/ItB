# Pilots Quick Reference

## Leveling System

- **XP Source:** 1 XP per Vek HP killed (e.g., 2 HP Hornet = 2 XP). Soldier Psion adds +1 HP/XP to all Vek.
- **Level 1:** 25 cumulative XP -- unlocks 1 random perk (choose from 2 offered).
- **Level 2:** 75 cumulative XP (50 more after Level 1) -- unlocks 1 more random perk.
- **Max XP:** 75. Pilots stop earning XP after reaching Level 2.
- **Indirect kills** (environmental, emerging Vek crushing): XP split among all pilots in squad.
- **Artificial Pilots** (default AI): Do not gain XP. No skills. Active when no pilot is assigned.
- **Time Travel:** One surviving pilot can be sent back, retaining XP and perks. Failed pilots appear as 0 XP recruits in future runs.
- **Death:** Pilot dies permanently if their mech is destroyed and not restored by end of battle.

### Perk Restrictions by Pilot

- **Ariadne:** Cannot learn +2 Mech HP, Skilled, +1 Mech Reactor, or Conservative.
- **Mafan:** Cannot learn +2 Mech HP, Skilled, Technician, Masochist, +1 Mech Reactor, or Conservative.
- **Cyborg Pilots:** Cannot learn Conservative.

## Secret Pilots (FTL Crossover)

Found via special encounters during missions. Trigger: break a marked mountain or ice tile (look for a small glowing dot) to reveal a "Strange Object." Collect the beacon, then protect the Strange Pod that drops from the sky until mission end. Permanently unlocked once found. Related achievement: "Distant Friends."

| Pilot | Race | Special Skill | Power | Notes |
|-------|------|---------------|-------|-------|
| Kazaaakpleth | Mantis | **Mantis:** 2 damage melee attack replaces Repair | -- | Cannot repair. Instead deals 2 melee damage to adjacent. High risk, high reward. |
| Mafan | Zoltan | **Zoltan:** +1 Reactor Core. Reduce Mech HP to 1. Gain Shield every turn | -- | Mech has 1 HP but regenerating shield each turn. Free reactor core. Excellent on Ice Mech. Many perk restrictions. |
| Ariadne | Rockman | **Rockman:** +3 Health and immune to Fire | -- | Massive HP boost + fire immunity. Best on self-damaging mechs or Flame Behemoths. Several perk restrictions. |

## CorPilots (Corporate Timeline Pilots)

- Work for the Corporations in the current timeline.
- Never have any special skills.
- The best available pair start every new timeline as your initial squad.
- Functionally equivalent to Artificial Pilots for bot purposes (no abilities, do gain XP).

## Pilot Skill Categories

### Movement
- **Lily Reed** -- Impulsive: +3 Move on first turn
- **Henry Kwan** -- Maneuverable: Move through enemies
- **Chen Rong** -- Sidestep: +1 tile movement after attacking
- **Archimedes** -- Fire-and-Forget: Move again after shooting (1 power)
- **Prospero** -- Flying: Grants flight (1 power)
- **Gana** -- Preemptive Strike: Deploy anywhere (1 power)

### Defensive
- **Bethany Jones** -- Starting Shield: Shield at mission start
- **Abe Isamu** -- Armored: Reduce damage by 1
- **Mafan** -- Zoltan: Shield every turn (but HP = 1)
- **Ariadne** -- Rockman: +3 HP and fire immunity
- **Isaac Jones** -- Temporal Reset: Extra undo per battle
- **Camila Vera** -- Evasion: Immune to Webbing and Smoke
- **Adam** -- Chosen One: Shield + Move on Reset Turn

### Offensive
- **Silica** -- Double Shot: Attack twice without moving (2 power)
- **Kazaaakpleth** -- Mantis: 2 damage melee replaces Repair
- **Morgan Lejeune** -- Field Research: Boosted on kill
- **Kai Miller** -- Arrogant Boost: Boosted at full HP

### Utility
- **Ralph Karlsson** -- Experienced: +2 bonus XP per kill (leveling speed)
- **Harold Schmidt** -- Frenzied Repair: Push adjacent tiles on repair
- **Rosie Rivets** -- Reassuring Hand: Heal adjacent mechs after moving
