# Vek Bestiary

Reference for all Vek enemy types in Into the Breach. For each entry: base stats, attack info, and Alpha variant differences.

---

## Common Vek (Base Game)

### Firefly
- **HP:** 3 | **Move:** 2 | **Category:** Common | **Type:** Ground
- **Attack:** Accelerating Thorax -- ranged projectile (straight line), 1 damage
- **Behavior:** Shoots in a direct line across the board, like Taurus Cannon without push. Low movement but dangerous range.
- **Alpha:** HP 5, Enhanced Thorax -- 3 damage

### Hornet
- **HP:** 2 | **Move:** 5 | **Category:** Common | **Type:** Flying
- **Attack:** Stinger -- melee, 1 damage to adjacent target
- **Behavior:** High mobility flyer. Immune to drowning. Can reach dangerous positions quickly.
- **Alpha:** HP 4, Launching Stinger -- 2 damage, hits 2 tiles in front

### Leaper
- **HP:** 1 | **Move:** 4 | **Category:** Common | **Type:** Ground (Jumper)
- **Attack:** Fangs -- melee, webs adjacent target then stabs for 3 damage on enemy turn
- **Behavior:** Jumps to destination (ignores obstacles in path). Webs target on arrival, preventing movement. Very high damage but only 1 HP. Cannot spawn alongside Scorpion.
- **Alpha:** HP 3, Sharpened Fangs -- 5 damage (with web)

### Scarab
- **HP:** 2 | **Move:** 3 | **Category:** Common | **Type:** Ground
- **Attack:** Spitting Glands -- artillery (5 tile range), 1 damage to single tile
- **Behavior:** Lobs shots over obstacles. Weakest common Vek. Easy to neutralize with any single mech.
- **Alpha:** HP 4, Spitting Glands -- 3 damage

### Scorpion
- **HP:** 3 | **Move:** 3 | **Category:** Common | **Type:** Ground
- **Attack:** Stinging Spinneret -- melee, webs adjacent target then stabs for 1 damage
- **Behavior:** Webs target to prevent movement, then attacks. Dangerous because webbing restricts your mech options. Cannot spawn alongside Leaper.
- **Alpha:** HP 5, Goring Spinneret -- webs then 3 damage

## Common Vek (Advanced Edition)

### Bouncer
- **HP:** 3 | **Move:** 3 | **Category:** Common | **Type:** Ground
- **Attack:** Energized Horns -- melee charge, 1 damage to target then bounces backwards
- **Behavior:** Charges at target, deals damage, then retreats back the way it came.
- **Alpha:** HP 4, Powered Horns -- 3 damage then bounce

### Gastropod
- **HP:** 3 | **Move:** 2 | **Category:** Common | **Type:** Ground
- **Attack:** Hooked Proboscis -- ranged grapple, 1 damage, pulls Vek toward objects or pulls units to Vek
- **Behavior:** Grapple attack that repositions units. Can pull targets into dangerous positions.
- **Alpha:** HP 4, Move 3, Barbed Proboscis -- 3 damage with grapple

### Mosquito
- **HP:** 2 | **Move:** 4 | **Category:** Common | **Type:** Flying
- **Attack:** Smoked Tentacles -- melee, smokes the target then whips for 1 damage
- **Behavior:** High mobility flyer. Applies smoke (disables weapons) to target before attacking.
- **Alpha:** HP 4, Storm Tentacles -- smokes then 3 damage

### Moth
- **HP:** 3 | **Move:** 3 | **Category:** Common | **Type:** Flying
- **Attack:** Repulsive Pellets -- artillery, 1 damage, pushes both shooter and target
- **Behavior:** Flying artillery that pushes itself backward when firing. Double displacement makes it tricky.
- **Alpha:** HP 5, Repugnant Pellets -- 3 damage, pushes both

## Rare Vek (Base Game)

### Beetle
- **HP:** 4 | **Move:** 2 | **Category:** Rare | **Type:** Ground
- **Attack:** Pincers -- charge forward in a line, 1 damage and push target
- **Behavior:** Charges across the board. Highest HP of any non-Alpha. Dies if charge goes over water/lava/chasm/acid. Can push other Vek into buildings or hazards.
- **Alpha:** HP 5, Sharpened Pincers -- 3 damage and push

### Blobber
- **HP:** 3 | **Move:** 2 | **Category:** Rare | **Type:** Ground
- **Attack:** Unstable Growths -- artillery, throws a Blob onto any aligned tile
- **Behavior:** Spawns Blobs (1 HP, explode for 1 adjacent damage) onto tiles in its row/column. Blob explodes last in attack order. Max 1 Blobber per mission. Cannot co-exist with Spider or Plasmodia.
- **Alpha:** HP 4, Volatile Growths -- throws Alpha Blob (explodes for 3 damage)

### Burrower
- **HP:** 3 | **Move:** 4 | **Category:** Rare | **Type:** Ground (Burrowing)
- **Attack:** Spiked Carapace -- melee, slams 3 tiles in a row for 1 damage each
- **Behavior:** Burrows underground after taking damage (becomes untargetable). Stable: cannot be pushed or teleported. High mobility.
- **Alpha:** HP 5, Bladed Carapace -- 2 damage to 3 tiles

### Centipede
- **HP:** 3 | **Move:** 2 | **Category:** Rare | **Type:** Ground
- **Attack:** Acidic Vomit -- ranged projectile (straight line), 1 damage + A.C.I.D. on target tile and 2 perpendicular tiles
- **Behavior:** Like Firefly but applies A.C.I.D. in a 3-tile cross pattern. Cannot appear on island 1. Max 2 (including Alpha) per mission. A.C.I.D. can be useful against other Vek.
- **Alpha:** HP 5, Corrosive Vomit -- 2 damage + A.C.I.D.

### Crab
- **HP:** 3 | **Move:** 3 | **Category:** Rare | **Type:** Ground
- **Attack:** Explosive Expulsions -- artillery (5 tile range), 1 damage to 2 tiles
- **Behavior:** Hits two tiles simultaneously with artillery. More dangerous than Scarab due to double targeting.
- **Alpha:** HP 5, Explosive Expulsions -- 3 damage to 2 tiles

### Digger
- **HP:** 2 | **Move:** 3 | **Category:** Rare | **Type:** Ground
- **Attack:** Digging Tusks -- melee, creates a rock wall behind itself then attacks adjacent tiles for 1 damage
- **Behavior:** Spawns a mountain tile before attacking. The rock wall can block movement paths but also provides cover.
- **Alpha:** HP 4, Digging Tusks -- 2 damage, still creates rock wall

### Spider
- **HP:** 2 | **Move:** 2 | **Category:** Rare | **Type:** Ground
- **Attack:** Tiny Offspring -- artillery, throws a Spiderling Egg onto any aligned tile
- **Behavior:** Egg webs all adjacent units on landing, hatches into Spiderling next turn. Max 1 Spider (including Alpha) per mission. Cannot spawn on island 1. Prioritizes webbing multiple targets.
- **Alpha:** HP 4, Large Offspring -- throws egg that hatches into Alpha Spiderling

## Rare Vek (Advanced Edition)

### Plasmodia
- **HP:** 3 | **Move:** 2 | **Category:** Rare | **Type:** Ground
- **Attack:** Splitting Spores -- artillery, launches a short-lived Spore
- **Behavior:** Spawns Spores that sacrifice themselves to fire a projectile (1 damage + push). Cannot co-exist with Spider or Blobber.
- **Alpha:** HP 5, Spawning Spores -- launches Alpha Spore (3 damage + push)

### Starfish
- **HP:** 2 | **Move:** 3 | **Category:** Rare | **Type:** Ground
- **Attack:** Brittle Appendages -- area, 1 damage to four diagonal tiles
- **Behavior:** Attacks on diagonals instead of cardinal directions. Unusual targeting pattern.
- **Alpha:** HP 4, Lacerated Appendages -- 2 damage to four diagonal tiles

### Tumblebug
- **HP:** 3 | **Move:** 3 | **Category:** Rare | **Type:** Ground
- **Attack:** Explosive Prize -- creates an explosive boulder, attacks it next turn for 1 damage (boulder explodes)
- **Behavior:** Two-turn attack cycle. Turn 1: places boulder. Turn 2: destroys boulder causing explosion. Boulder can be moved/destroyed by player.
- **Alpha:** HP 5, Explosive Prize -- attacks boulder for 3 damage

## Special Vek

### Volatile Vek
- **HP:** 4 | **Move:** 3 | **Category:** Objective | **Type:** Ground
- **Attack:** Stinging Spinneret -- melee, webs adjacent target then stabs for 1 damage
- **Special:** Explosive Decay -- explodes on death, dealing 1 damage to adjacent tiles
- **Behavior:** Like a Scorpion but explodes on death. Appears in specific missions as an objective target. Must be killed carefully to avoid collateral damage.

- **Attack:** Unstable Guts -- self-destructs, 1 damage to adjacent tiles. Attacks last in enemy order.
- **Note:** Spawned by Blobber. Kill before it explodes.

### Alpha Blob
- **HP:** 1 | **Move:** 0 | **Type:** Stationary
- **Attack:** Volatile Guts -- self-destructs, 3 damage to adjacent tiles.
- **Note:** Spawned by Alpha Blobber. High priority kill target.

### Blob Leader
- **HP:** 2 | **Move:** 0 | **Type:** Stationary
- **Attack:** Eruptive Guts -- self-destructs for 1 damage to itself and 2 damage to adjacent tiles.
- **Note:** Spawned by Blobber Leader.

### Spiderling Egg
- **HP:** 1 | **Move:** 0 | **Type:** Stationary
- **Attack:** Hatches into Spiderling next turn. Webs adjacent units on landing.

### Spiderling
- **HP:** 1 | **Move:** 3 | **Type:** Ground
- **Attack:** Tiny Mandibles -- melee, 1 damage to adjacent target
- **Note:** Weak but numerous. Hatched from Spiderling Eggs.

### Spore (Advanced Edition)
- **HP:** 1 | **Move:** 0 | **Type:** Stationary
- **Attack:** Scarred Secretion -- sacrifices self to fire projectile, 1 damage + push target
- **Note:** Spawned by Plasmodia. Short-lived.

### Alpha Spore (Advanced Edition)
- **HP:** 1 | **Move:** 0 | **Type:** Stationary
- **Attack:** Hemorrhaged Secretion -- sacrifices self, 3 damage + push target
- **Note:** Spawned by Alpha Plasmodia.

### Vicious Spore (Advanced Edition)

## Psions

All Psions: HP 2 | Move 2 | Flying. They provide passive auras to all other Vek while alive. Kill priority is always high.

### Soldier Psion
- **Aura:** Invigorating Spores -- all other Vek receive +1 HP while Psion lives
- **Impact:** Effectively increases HP of every Vek on the board. Kill to remove bonus HP.

### Blood Psion
- **Aura:** Regeneration -- all other Vek heal 1 HP at the start of their turn
- **Impact:** Wounded Vek recover. Makes attrition strategies useless until Psion dies.

### Shell Psion
- **Aura:** Hardened Carapace -- all other Vek take 1 less weapon damage
- **Impact:** Effectively gives all Vek +1 armor. Low-damage attacks become useless. High priority kill.

### Blast Psion
- **Aura:** Explosive Decay -- all other Vek explode on death (1 damage to adjacent tiles)
- **Impact:** Killing Vek near buildings causes collateral damage. Must position kills carefully.

### Psion Tyrant
- **Aura:** Hive Targeting -- all player units take 1 damage at end of every turn
- **Impact:** Constant unavoidable damage to all mechs. Extremely high kill priority.

### Smoldering Psion (Advanced Edition)
- **Aura:** Burning Carapace -- all other Vek are immune to Fire and leave Fire on death
- **Impact:** Vek cannot be killed or damaged by fire. Counters Flame Behemoths and fire-based strategies.

### Arachnid Psion (Advanced Edition)
- **Aura:** Arachnid Host -- all other Vek spawn a Spiderling Egg on death
- **Impact:** Every Vek kill spawns an egg that webs nearby units and hatches into a Spiderling.

### Raging Psion (Advanced Edition)
- **Aura:** Raging Hormones -- all other Vek are Boosted (+1 damage to attacks)
- **Impact:** Every Vek attack deals 1 extra damage. Buildings become much more fragile.

## Leaders

Boss-tier Vek that appear in specific missions. All Leaders have the Massive trait (can walk in water but cannot attack/repair in water) unless noted.

### Beetle Leader
- **HP:** 6 | **Move:** 3 | **Type:** Ground (Massive)
- **Attack:** Flaming Abdomen -- charge, 3 damage + push target, sets every tile in charge path on Fire
- **Behavior:** Like Beetle but much stronger. Fire trail creates persistent hazards.

### Firefly Leader
- **HP:** 6 | **Move:** 3 | **Type:** Ground (Massive)
- **Attack:** Burning Thorax -- fires projectiles in TWO directions, 4 damage each
- **Behavior:** Attacks two targets simultaneously. Extremely dangerous.

### Hornet Leader
- **HP:** 6 | **Move:** 3 | **Type:** Flying
- **Attack:** Super Stinger -- stabs 3 tiles in a row for 2 damage each
- **Behavior:** Flying boss with wide melee attack. Hits 3 consecutive tiles.

### Scorpion Leader
- **HP:** 7 | **Move:** 3 | **Type:** Ground (Massive)
- **Attack:** Massive Spinneret -- webs ALL adjacent targets, 2 damage to adjacent tiles
- **Behavior:** Highest HP Leader. Webs everything around it then attacks all adjacent tiles.

### Spider Leader
- **HP:** 6 | **Move:** 2 | **Type:** Ground (Massive, Jumper)
- **Attack:** Plentiful Offspring -- throws 2-3 Spiderling Eggs
- **Behavior:** Massive egg spam. Can overwhelm the board with spiderlings.

### Blobber Leader (Advanced Edition)
- **HP:** 5 | **Move:** 2
- **Attack:** Eruptive Growths -- throws a sticky Blob Leader (2 HP, 2 adj damage on explode)

### Bouncer Leader (Advanced Edition)
- **HP:** 4 | **Move:** 3
- **Attack:** Sweeping Horns -- 2 damage to and push 3 tiles then bounce backwards

### Centipede Leader (Advanced Edition)
- **HP:** 7 | **Move:** 3
- **Attack:** Caustic Vomit -- 3 damage projectile, applies A.C.I.D. on nearby units and all tiles in flight path

### Crab Leader (Advanced Edition)
- **HP:** 6 | **Move:** 3
- **Attack:** Raining Expulsions -- 2 damage artillery + 1 damage to every tile in projectile path

### Gastropod Leader (Advanced Edition)
- **HP:** 6 | **Move:** 3
- **Attack:** Flaming Proboscis -- 3 damage grapple + sets nearby tiles on Fire

### Mosquito Leader (Advanced Edition)
- **HP:** 5 | **Move:** 4
- **Attack:** Cloudburst Tentacles -- smokes and webs target, then kills target outright

### Plasmodia Leader (Advanced Edition)
- **HP:** 5 | **Move:** 2
- **Attack:** Generating Spores -- launches a Vicious Spore (2 HP, 2 damage + push)

### Scarab Leader (Advanced Edition)
- **HP:** 6 | **Move:** 3
- **Attack:** Expectorating Glands -- 4 damage artillery to single tile, pushes adjacent tiles

### Starfish Leader (Advanced Edition)
- **HP:** 6 | **Move:** 3
- **Attack:** Scored Appendages -- 3 damage to 4 diagonal tiles + pushes 4 cardinal tiles

### Tumblebug Leader (Advanced Edition)
- **HP:** 6 | **Move:** 3
- **Attack:** Explosive Prizes -- creates TWO explosive boulders, attacks one for 3 damage next turn

## Bosses & Special

### Psion Abomination
- **HP:** 5 | **Move:** 3 | **Type:** Flying
- **Aura:** Overpowered -- all other Vek gain +1 HP, Regeneration, AND explode on death
- **Behavior:** Combines Soldier Psion + Blood Psion + Blast Psion effects. Absolute top kill priority.

### Large Goo
- **HP:** 3 | **Move:** 3 | **Type:** Ground (Massive)
- **Attack:** Goo Attack -- melee, 4 damage to adjacent tile
- **Special:** Spawns 2 Medium Goos on death

### Medium Goo
- **HP:** 2 | **Move:** 3
- **Attack:** Goo Attack -- melee, 4 damage to adjacent tile
- **Special:** Spawns 2 Small Goos on death

### Small Goo
- **HP:** 1 | **Move:** 3
- **Attack:** Goo Attack -- melee, 4 damage to adjacent tile
- **Behavior:** Final stage. Dies normally without spawning more.

**Goo Strategy:** Large Goo splits into 2 Medium (which split into 2 Small each), potentially creating 7 total enemies. Try to kill over water/chasms to prevent splitting, or manage the cascade carefully.

## Quick Reference: Threat Priority

**Immediate threats (building damage):** Firefly > Scorpion > Leaper > Beetle > Crab > Scarab
**Support threats (kill first):** Psion Tyrant > Shell Psion > Raging Psion > Blood Psion > Soldier Psion > Blast Psion
**Nuisance (manage):** Spider > Blobber > Burrower > Hornet > Mosquito
**Boss tier:** Psion Abomination > Scorpion Leader > Firefly Leader > Beetle Leader

## Quick Reference: Movement Types

| Type | Trait | Water | Chasm | Mountains |
|------|-------|-------|-------|-----------|
| Ground | Normal | Dies | Dies | Blocked |
| Flying | Hornet, Moth, Mosquito, all Psions | Passes | Passes | Passes |
| Jumper | Leaper, Spider Leader | Passes | Passes | Passes |
| Burrowing | Burrower | Blocked | Blocked | Blocked |
| Massive | Leaders | Walks in (no attack) | Dies | Blocked |
