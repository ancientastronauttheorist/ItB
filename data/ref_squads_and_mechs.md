| Mech | Class | HP | Move | Weapon | Damage | Effect |
|------|-------|----|------|--------|--------|--------|
| Combat Mech | Prime | 3 | 3 | Titan Fist | 2 | Punch adjacent tile, damage + push |
| Cannon Mech | Brute | 3 | 3 | Taurus Cannon | 1 | Projectile, damage + push target |
| Artillery Mech | Ranged | 2 | 3 | Artemis Artillery | 1 | Artillery strike, damage tile + push adjacent |

**Weapon Upgrades:**

- Taurus Cannon: U1: +1 Damage (1 core) | U2: +1 Damage (1 core)
- Artemis Artillery: U1: Buildings Immune (1 core) | U2: +2 Damage (1 core)

**Squad Achievements:**
- Watery Grave: Drown 3 enemies in water in a single battle
- Ramming Speed: Kill an enemy 5+ tiles away with a Dash Punch
- Island Secure: Complete the first Corporate Island

**Strategy Notes:** Basic, straightforward squad. Lacks mobility -- invest in +1 Move or Dash. Weapons are generic so pilots/weapons swap freely. Strong against 2-HP enemies. Weak mid-game; Shell Psion and Bot Leader are hard counters until upgraded.

| Pulse Mech | Science | 3 | 4 | Repulse | 0 | Push all adjacent tiles |

**Passive:** Storm Generator (on Rocket Mech) -- All Smoke deals 1 damage to enemies each turn. U1: +1 Damage (1 core).

**Special:** Jet Mech has Flying.

**Weapon Upgrades:**
- Aerial Bombs: U1: +1 Damage (1 core) | U2: +1 Range -- jump over + attack additional target (2 cores)

Don't spread smoke carelessly -- lasts entire battle. Camila Vera is the best pilot (immune to smoke). Pulse Mech Shield Self is a strong early unlock for spawn blocking.

| Defense Mech | Science | 2 | 4 | Attraction Pulse / Shield Projector | 0 / 0 | Pull target 1 tile / Shield tiles (2 uses/battle) |

**Weapon Upgrades:**
- Burst Beam: U1: Ally Immune (2 cores) | U2: +1 Damage -- starting damage becomes 4 (1 core)
- Ramming Engines: U1: +1 Damage Each -- +1 to self and target (1 core) | U2: +1 Damage -- target only (1 core)

## Blitzkrieg
**Affiliation:** Detritus Disposal A | **Unlock:** 3 Coins

| Mech | Class | HP | Move | Weapon | Damage | Effect |
|------|-------|----|------|--------|--------|--------|
| Lightning Mech | Prime | 3 | 3 | Electric Whip | 2 | Chain damage through adjacent targets |
| Hook Mech | Brute | 3 | 3 | Grappling Hook | 0 | Pull mech towards objects, or units to mech |
| Boulder Mech | Ranged | 2 | 3 | Rock Accelerator | 2 | Launch rock at tile, push adjacent tiles |

**Special:** Hook Mech has Armored (weapon damage reduced by 1). Electric Whip requires 1 core to power.

**Weapon Upgrades:**
- Electric Whip: U1: Building Chain -- chain through buildings without damage (1 core) | U2: +1 Damage to all tiles (1 core)
- Grappling Hook: U1: Shield Ally -- shields ally when used on them (1 core)
- Rock Accelerator: U1: +1 Damage (1 core)

**Squad Achievements:**
- Chain Attack: Chain Whip through 10 tiles
- Lightning War: Finish first 2 Corporate Islands in under 30 minutes
- Hold the Line: Block 4 emerging Vek in a single turn

**Strategy Notes:** Electric Whip is extremely powerful but damages everything -- own mechs, objectives, allies. Hook and Boulder are for positioning to minimize collateral. Boulders block spawns, chain whip, and block projectiles. Hook Mech (armored) can serve as chain fodder but Boulder Mech (2 HP) dies instantly. Avoid entity-based mission objectives. Networked Shielding passive is best-in-slot. Key pilots: Silica (double whip), Mafan (instant Building Chain).

**Special:** Ice Mech has Flying. Cryo-Launcher requires 1 core to power.

**Weapon Upgrades:**
- Spartan Shield: U1: Gain Shield when bashing (1 core) | U2: +1 Damage (1 core)
- Janus Cannon: U1: +1 Damage (1 core) | U2: +1 Damage (1 core)

- Cryo Expert: Shoot Cryo-Launcher 4 times in a single battle
- Trick Shot: Kill 3 enemies with a single Janus Cannon attack

Frozen satellites won't launch (fails objective). Key pilots: Mafan (immune to self-freeze), Silica (double Janus Cannon shots), Kazaaakpleth (attack while frozen).

## Secret Squad
**Affiliation:** Secret | **Unlock:** 25 Coins (requires unlocking all 8 named squads first)

| Mech | Class | HP | Move | Weapon | Damage | Effect |
|------|-------|----|------|--------|--------|--------|
| Techno-Beetle | Cyborg | 3 | 3 | Ramming Speed | 1 | Dash across map, damage + push target |
| Techno-Hornet | Cyborg | 2 | 4 | Needle Shot | 1 | Throw needles, push furthest tile (starts 1-tile range) |
| Techno-Scarab | Cyborg | 2 | 3 | Explosive Goo | 1 | Artillery, damage tile + push adjacent away |

**Special:** Techno-Hornet has Flying. All mechs are Cyborg class (non-Cyborg weapons cost +1 core). Pilots are fixed Cyborg Pilots that cannot be swapped. Cyborg Pilots lose 25 XP on death instead of dying.

**Weapon Upgrades:**
- Ramming Speed: U1: Smoke Behind -- tile behind gains Smoke (1 core) | U2: +2 Damage (1 core)
- Needle Shot: U1: Range & Damage -- +1 tile, +1 damage to all (1 core) | U2: Range & Damage -- +1 tile, +1 damage to all (1 core)
- Explosive Goo: U1: +1 Tile -- area +1 (1 core) | U2: +2 Damage (1 core)

**Squad Achievements:**
- None (Secret Squad has no achievements)

**Strategy Notes:** Most challenging squad. Cyborg class means all other weapons cost +1 core. Cannot use time traveller. Needle Shot scales best (1 to 4 net damage with 2 cores) -- prioritize giving it reactor cores. Getting a new weapon through perfect island is critical. Needle Shot and Explosive Goo become very strong late-game. Replace Ramming Speed weapon first (scales worst). Avoid advanced pilot abilities (dilutes skill pool, reduces chance of +1 Mech Reactor).
